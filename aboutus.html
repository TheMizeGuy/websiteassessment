My restaurant name is Le Meisl based off of our families last name. It is an Italian restaurant with a very comfortable almost home like atmosphere while still maintaining the classy Italian vibe. With vibrant colors and fantastic liking you're sure to love our atmosphere

We serve a lot of signature pasta dishes as well as things like chicken marsala and chicken francese. We have an extensive wine selection as well for our customers to choose from. The restaurant has been in the family for generations and my goal is to modernize things(we have an online menu during the ongoing pandemic).

The restaurant is large and is also situated on a lot of land and includes a banquet hall so we also service large wedding parties particulary in the fall season. Our mission statement is Ci vediamo!(until we meet again) because we know you'll have such a good experience with us you'll be sure to come back soon!

  4. Multiple wedding venue selections
